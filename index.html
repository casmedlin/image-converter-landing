Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2026

Image Converter ("the App") is a desktop application that processes images entirely on your device. This website and the App together ("the Service") are committed to your privacy.

1. No Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage statistics, or analytics. All image processing occurs locally on your device. No images, metadata, or derived files are ever sent to any external server.

2. Website

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and fetches release information from the public GitHub API. We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics. Cloudflare may process standard request logs (IP address, user agent, timestamp) as part of their CDN service. These logs are not accessible to us and are retained per Cloudflare's policy.

3. Local Processing Only

All image conversion, resizing, and format transformation is performed using local system tools. The App has no network access and no cloud components.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It operates fully offline and does not require an internet connection. The website uses GitHub's API solely to display download links for the latest release.

5. Data Storage

Temporary files created during processing are stored in your system's temporary directory and are deleted immediately after processing completes. Output files are saved only to the location you explicitly choose.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data. Since we collect no personal data, no action is needed. If you have questions, contact us at info@casmedlin.com.

If this policy is updated, the effective date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any changes.
